Telangana: The Land of Festivals

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Telangana is a culturally rich state in southern India, known for its vibrant traditions, historic landmarks, and delicious cuisine. With its mix of modern cities and ancient heritage, Telangana offers visitors a unique experience. The state's capital, Hyderabad, is famous for its historic sites, while places like Warangal and Ramoji Film City showcase the rich history and entertainment of the region.
